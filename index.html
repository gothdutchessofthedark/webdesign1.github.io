Everything Comes

By Thomas Hardy

"The house is bleak and cold
Built so new for me! All the winds upon the wold
Search it through for me;
No screening trees abount,
Keep on view for me"

"My Love, I am planting trees
As a screen for you,
Both form winds, and eyes that tease,
And peer in for you.
Only wait till they have grown,
As I mean for you."

"Then I will bear it, Love,
And will wait," she said.
- So, with years, there grew a grove.
"Skill how great!" she said.
"As you wished, Dear?" - "Yes, I see!
But - I'm dying; and for me 'Tis too late," she said.
